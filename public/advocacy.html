What is Advocacy?

An advocate provides you with the information and support you need for better aged care. NGO helps tens of thousands of older people resolve their issues with government-funded aged care services each year.Our national team of advocates is here to ensure your rights are upheld and your wishes are met, whether you are living independently at home or in residential care.
Our services are free, independent and confidential.
